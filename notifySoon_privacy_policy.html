1. Information We Collect

1.1 Information You Provide to Us

Account Information (Optional):

When you sign in with Google, we collect your name, email address, and profile picture from your Google account. You can use the app without signing in; all features work offline with local storage.

Warranty Information:

  • Product name, brand, and category
  • Owner name
  • Purchase date, price, and retailer name
  • Warranty duration and expiration dates
  • Serial numbers and model information
  • Custom notes and fields
  • Photos of warranty documents and receipts

Vehicle Service Information:

  • Vehicle owner name, brand, model, and year
  • Registration number, engine number, and chassis number
  • Insurance expiry date
  • Service history including dates, odometer readings, service center details
  • Service center contact information
  • Service notes and reminders

Documents and Images:

  • Photos captured via camera or selected from your device gallery
  • Scanned warranty documents and receipts
  • Text extracted from images using on-device OCR technology

1.2 Information Collected Automatically

Device Information:

  • Device type and model information
  • Operating system version
  • Unique device identifiers (for push notifications only)
  • Network connectivity status

Firebase Cloud Messaging (FCM) Tokens:

We collect FCM tokens to send you warranty expiration and service reminder notifications.

2. How We Use Your Information

We use the information we collect to:

  • Provide Core Functionality: Store and manage your warranty and vehicle service records
  • Send Reminders: Deliver push notifications for upcoming warranty expirations and vehicle service dates (1, 7, 15, 20, 30 days before)
  • Cloud Backup: Enable optional backup and restore of your data to your personal Google Drive
  • Document Scanning: Process images locally using on-device OCR to extract warranty information
  • Authentication: Verify your identity when using Google Sign-In for cloud features
  • App Improvement: Understand network connectivity to provide offline functionality

3. Data Storage and Security

3.1 Local Storage

  • Primary Storage: All your warranty and vehicle data is stored locally on your device using secure AsyncStorage
  • Documents: Images and documents are stored in your device's local file system
  • Offline Access: Your data is always available offline; internet is only required for Google Drive backup and Google Sign-In

3.2 Cloud Storage (Optional)

  • Google Drive Backup: When you choose to backup data, it is stored in your personal Google Drive account in a private app-specific folder (appDataFolder)
  • User Control: You control when to backup and restore individual items
  • Data Format: Backups are stored as JSON files with naming format: {name}{field}{itemId}_{date}_{timestamp}.json
  • Privacy: Backup data remains in your personal Google Drive account and is not accessible to us

3.3 Firebase Services

Limited Use: We only use Firebase Firestore to store FCM tokens for push notification delivery

No Comprehensive Tracking: We do not store your warranty or vehicle data on our servers

3.4 Security Measures

  • Local data is stored using AsyncStorage (standard mobile storage)
  • Google Drive backups are protected by your Google account security (use strong passwords and two-factor authentication)
  • We do not implement custom encryption for local storage
  • No data is transmitted to our servers beyond Firebase notification tokens

6. Your Privacy Rights and Choices

6.1 Access and Control

  • View Your Data: All your data is stored locally on your device and accessible within the app
  • Edit or Delete: You can edit or delete any warranty or vehicle record at any time
  • Export Data: You can backup your data to your Google Drive
  • Account Deletion: You can sign out from Google Sign-In at any time; this removes cloud sync but retains local data

6.2 Permissions Control

You can control app permissions through your device settings:

Android: Camera (required for document scanning), Storage (required to save and access documents), Notifications (required for warranty reminders), Internet (required for Google Sign-In and Drive backup)

iOS: Camera (required for document scanning), Photos (required to select existing images), Notifications (required for warranty reminders)

You can revoke these permissions, but some app features may not function properly.

6.3 Opt-Out Options

  • Google Sign-In: You can use the app without signing in (skip login)
  • Cloud Backup: Backup to Google Drive is entirely optional
  • Notifications: You can disable push notifications in your device settings
  • OCR Scanning: You can manually enter warranty information instead of using camera scanning

7. Data Retention

  • Google Drive Backups: Remain in your Google Drive until you delete them
  • FCM Tokens: Stored in Firebase Firestore and remain until you sign out or uninstall the app
  • Account Data: Google Sign-In data is managed by Google's retention policies
