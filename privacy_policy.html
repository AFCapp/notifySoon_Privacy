Introduction

Welcome to NotifySoon. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you use our mobile application. Please read this privacy policy carefully. If you do not agree with the terms of this privacy policy, please do not access the application.

Developer Contact Information:

1. Information We Collect

1.1 Information You Provide to Us

Account Information (Optional):

When you sign in with Google, we collect your name, email address, and profile picture from your Google account. You can use the app without signing in; all features work offline with local storage.

Warranty Information:

  • Product name, brand, and owner name
  • Purchase date, price, and retailer name
  • Warranty duration and expiration dates
  • Serial numbers
  • Custom notes
  • Photos of warranty documents and receipts

Vehicle Service Information:

  • Vehicle owner name, brand, model, and year of manufacture
  • Registration number, engine number, and chassis number
  • Service history including dates, odometer readings, service center details
  • Service center contact information and service costs
  • Service notes and next service dates

Event Information:

  • Event name (e.g., birthdays, anniversaries, meetings)
  • Event date
  • Event description and notes
  • Event images from your device gallery
  • Recurring event preferences (yearly repeat option)

Documents and Images:

  • Photos captured via camera or selected from your device gallery
  • Scanned warranty documents and receipts
  • Text extracted from images using on-device OCR technology (Google ML Kit)

1.2 Information Collected Automatically

Device Information:

  • Device type and model information
  • Operating system version
  • Unique device identifiers (for push notifications only)

Notification Tokens:

We collect notification tokens to send you warranty expiration, service reminder, and event reminder notifications locally on your device.

2. How We Use Your Information

We use the information we collect to:

  • Provide Core Functionality: Store and manage your warranty records, vehicle service records, and event reminders
  • Send Reminders: Deliver local notifications for upcoming warranty expirations and vehicle service dates (1, 7, 15, 20, 30 days before), and event reminders (5, 4, 3, 2, 1 days before)
  • Cloud Backup: Enable optional backup and restore of your data to your personal Google Drive
  • Document Scanning: Process images locally using on-device OCR (Google ML Kit) to extract warranty information
  • Authentication: Verify your identity when using Google Sign-In for cloud features

3. Data Storage and Security

3.1 Local Storage

  • Primary Storage: All your warranty, vehicle, and event data is stored locally on your device using secure AsyncStorage
  • Documents: Images and documents are stored in your device's local file system
  • Offline Access: Your data is always available offline; internet is only required for Google Drive backup and Google Sign-In

3.2 Cloud Storage (Optional)

  • Google Drive Backup: When you choose to backup data, it is stored in your personal Google Drive account in a private app-specific folder (appDataFolder)
  • User Control: You control when to backup and restore individual items
  • Data Format: Backups are stored as JSON files with naming format: {name}{field}{itemId}_{date}_{timestamp}.json
  • Privacy: Backup data remains in your personal Google Drive account and is not accessible to us

3.3 Security Measures

  • Local data is stored using AsyncStorage (standard mobile storage)
  • Google Drive backups are protected by your Google account security (use strong passwords and two-factor authentication)
  • We do not implement custom encryption for local storage
  • No data is transmitted to our servers; all processing happens locally on your device

6. Permissions Required

NotifySoon requires the following permissions:

6.1 Camera Permission (android.permission.CAMERA)

  • Purpose: To allow you to take photos of warranty cards, bills, and documents for scanning
  • Usage: Used only when you tap "Take Photo" in the document scanner
  • Data Storage: Photos are processed locally using Google ML Kit OCR and stored on your device

6.2 Photo Selection (Android Photo Picker)

  • Purpose: To allow you to select existing photos from your gallery for document scanning and event images
  • Usage: Used only when you tap "From Gallery" in the document scanner or add images to events
  • Privacy: We use Android's system Photo Picker, which means the app does not have broad access to your photo library. You explicitly choose which specific photos to share with the app — we only receive the photos you select

6.3 Notification Permission

  • Purpose: To send you local reminder notifications for warranty expiries, upcoming vehicle services, and event reminders
  • Usage: Notifications are scheduled locally on your device — warranty and vehicle reminders (1, 7, 15, 20, and 30 days before expiry/service dates), event reminders (5, 4, 3, 2, and 1 days before event dates)

6.4 Internet Access

  • Purpose: Only used for Google Drive integration (backup and restore features) and Google Sign-In
  • Usage: Only when you explicitly use the backup, restore, or sign-in features

7. Your Privacy Rights and Choices

7.1 Access and Control

  • View Your Data: All your data is stored locally on your device and accessible within the app
  • Edit or Delete: You can edit or delete any warranty, vehicle, or event record at any time
  • Export Data: You can backup your data to your Google Drive
  • Account Deletion: You can sign out from Google Sign-In at any time; this removes cloud sync but retains local data

7.2 Permissions Control

You can control app permissions through your device settings:

Android: Settings > Apps > NotifySoon > Permissions

iOS: Settings > NotifySoon > Permissions

You can revoke these permissions, but some app features may not function properly.

7.3 Opt-Out Options

  • Google Sign-In: You can use the app without signing in (skip login)
  • Cloud Backup: Backup to Google Drive is entirely optional
  • Notifications: You can disable push notifications in your device settings
  • OCR Scanning: You can manually enter warranty information instead of using camera scanning

8. Data Retention

  • Local Data: Remains on your device until you manually delete it or uninstall the app
  • Google Drive Backups: Remain in your Google Drive until you delete them
  • Account Data: Google Sign-In data is managed by Google's retention policies
